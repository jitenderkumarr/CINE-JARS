⭐ 3/5

Cronin fails to rise to the occasion.

21 April 2023
After all the hype, the rave reviews, the revealing trailers, and the silly reaction videos (God, how I hate those), Evil Dead Rise turns out to be exactly what I expected: a serviceable sequel, nothing more, nothing less. Like the remake before it, it delivers on the gore, but is lacking in originality and likeable characters. The film takes place in a rundown apartment building, home to single mother Ellie (Alyssa Sutherland) and her three obnoxious brats, Bridget (Gabrielle Echols), Danny (Morgan Davies) and Kassie (Nell Fisher).
⭐ 4/5

Evil Dead Sink

13 May 2023
First, I am a big fan of all the previous movies, but this one is weak. The cast is bland. I didn't root for any of the female characters. To add, I was wondering around 30 minutes about the sex of the "DJ".... Need to check on Imdb lol. Second, a 4-5 years old as a bad ass looked pretty bad. Terrible idea. Third, the original and even the remake, had a great script and a great execution. Here everything looked overacted. Even the CGI effects looked cheap and the excessive gore doesn't improve the lack of intensity or interest.
